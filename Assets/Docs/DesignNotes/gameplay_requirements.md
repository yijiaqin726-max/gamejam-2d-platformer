# Gameplay Requirements

## Map 01: Forest

Music: `Assets/Audio/BGM/bgm_scene_01.wav`

Theme: forest.

Gameplay:

- Tutorial level.
- Keep the route simple.
- Player mainly jumps up and down across hill platforms.
- When the player meets a tree that cannot be jumped over, the player transforms into a leaf and flies upward.
- After flying into the sky, birds appear.
- In leaf form, the player must control movement to avoid birds.

## Map 02: Blue Temple

Music: `Assets/Audio/BGM/bgm_scene_02.wav`

Theme: blue temple.

Story transition:

- After the leaf drifts down, the player arrives at this map.

Gameplay:

- Add light orbs as guidance.
- Use collectible light orbs to lead the player forward.
- There is a pit in the middle of the route.
- The player must push a stone into the pit and use it as a foothold.

## Map 03: Cliff

Music: `Assets/Audio/BGM/bgm_scene_03.wav`

Theme: cliff.

Gameplay:

- Player transforms into vine form.
- Vines are distributed across the cliff.
- Vine form can climb upward.
- When the player reaches a higher vine, transform into that higher vine and continue climbing.

## Map 04: Cave

Music: `Assets/Audio/BGM/bgm_scene_04.wav`

Theme: cave.

Gameplay:

- Player transforms into stone form.
- Stone form smashes stalactites below.
- At the end of the cave, the player transforms into an upper vine.
- The player swings away like monkey-bar movement.
- After leaving the cave, the player continues climbing.
- The player climbs to the mountain top and enters the house.
